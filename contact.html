Frequently Asked Questions

The best time to visit Hassan is from October to February when the weather is pleasant for sightseeing. The temperature ranges between 15°C to 28°C during these months.

We recommend at least 2-3 days to cover the major attractions like Belur, Halebidu, and Shravanabelagola. If you want to explore coffee plantations and nature spots, consider 4-5 days.

Yes, KSRTC buses connect all major tourist destinations. However, for more convenience and flexibility, we recommend hiring a taxi or joining organized tours.

Belur and Halebid have limited accommodation options. Most visitors stay in Hassan city which has a range of hotels from budget to luxury, and make day trips to the temples.
