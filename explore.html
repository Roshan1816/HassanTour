Belur

4.5 (120 reviews)

The Chennakesava Temple in Belur is a magnificent example of Hoysala architecture, built in the 12th century. The temple is renowned for its intricate carvings and sculptures that depict scenes from Hindu epics.

Open: 7:30 AM - 8:30 PM
Entry: Free
Distance: 38km from Hassan
