Hassan Travel Guide

Everything you need to know for planning your perfect trip to Hassan

Best Time to Visit

Winter (Oct-Feb)

15°C - 28°C

Perfect weather for sightseeing. Peak tourist season.

Best for temple visits and outdoor activities

Summer (Mar-May)

22°C - 35°C

Warm days, cooler mornings. Fewer crowds.

Good for early morning visits

Monsoon (Jun-Sep)

20°C - 30°C

Lush green landscapes. Occasional heavy rains.

Beautiful for photography

How to Reach Hassan

By Air

Nearest airports:

  • Mysuru Airport (120km)
  • Mangaluru Airport (165km)
  • Bengaluru Airport (180km)

By Train

Hassan Junction is well connected to:

  • Bengaluru (3hrs)
  • Mysuru (2.5hrs)
  • Mangaluru (5hrs)

By Road

Excellent road connectivity via:

  • NH-75 (Bengaluru-Mangaluru)
  • SH-57 (Mysuru-Hassan)
  • SH-15 (Tumakuru-Hassan)

Getting Around Hassan
